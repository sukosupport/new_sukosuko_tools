Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy sets out how "SHOWROOM New Sukosuko Tools" (hereinafter referred to as "our App") handles information related to the privacy and personal information of users in the services it provides.

1. Information We Collect

Our App collects the following information to support users in utilizing the live streaming app SHOWROOM (hereafter, "SHOWROOM") more conveniently:

  • Usage status and log information (reports from app store operators such as Google and Apple)

Note that our App does not manage or collect any account-related information (login IDs, passwords, user information, etc.) or personal information. User authentication is performed through SHOWROOM's official API.

2. Use of Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • To improve the provision and operation of services
  • User support
  • Development of new features

3. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

Our App will not share or disclose user information to third parties, except in the following cases:

  • When disclosure is required by legal obligations

4. User Rights

Since our App does not manage any account information or personally identifiable information other than communication through SHOWROOM's official API, the rights users can exercise against our App are primarily as follows:

  • Access to Information: As our App does not directly collect personal information from users, the right to access depends on SHOWROOM's official services. Users can request access to their information in accordance with SHOWROOM's privacy policy.
  • Deletion of Information: Information stored by our App does not identify individuals. Uninstalling our App will stop the collection of information by our App.
  • Objections: If users have concerns about the functions of our App or the information collected, they can provide feedback at any time. Feedback should be sent to suko.assist@gmail.com.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our App uses cookies for the purposes of managing users' login states and streamlining communication with SHOWROOM's official API. These cookies temporarily store information on users' devices to maintain login states and facilitate smooth API communication when accessing SHOWROOM services.

The use of cookies by our App is limited to the following purposes:

  • Maintaining Login States: Maintaining session-based login states when users log into SHOWROOM.
  • Streamlining Communication with SHOWROOM's Official API: Ensuring safe and efficient communication between users and SHOWROOM's official services.

Our App does not use cookies or tracking technologies for any purpose other than communication with SHOWROOM's official API.

User Control over Cookies

Users can reset cookies and settings from the app's settings menu.

6. Protective Measures

Our App takes appropriate physical, technical, and administrative security measures to protect the safety of user information.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

Our App may update this Privacy Policy as necessary. The updated privacy policy will take effect upon its publication on our App or related official websites.
